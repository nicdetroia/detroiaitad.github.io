Why This Matters

Three problems most IT teams underestimate.

When a company retires hardware, they typically think about cost. They should be thinking about data breach exposure, legal liability, and the revenue they're leaving on the table. We fix all three.

Risk 01 — Data Security

One lost hard drive can cost millions.

HIPAA, GDPR, and state-level regulations have made data breach fines severe and public. A wiped device is only secure if the wipe is documented, certified, and performed to a recognized standard.

Our answer → Military-grade wipe + Certificate of Destruction
Risk 02 — Legal Compliance

Improper disposal is a PR and legal event.

Global environmental laws make it illegal to dump electronics without proper documentation. Most companies have no idea what happens to their hardware after it leaves the building. We do — and we can prove it.

Our answer → EPA-certified disposal + Certificate of Recycling
Risk 03 — Lost Value

Your "old" hardware has real market value.

Most companies throw away working GPUs, CPUs, and RAM that have a strong secondary market. We identify, test, and remarket those components — and share the revenue with you.

Our answer → IT Asset Remarketing + Revenue Share Statement
02
The Fortress Process

How we handle it — from your loading dock to final disposition.

Every step is logged, tracked, and documented. You get visibility the whole way through.

1

Secure Logistics

GPS-tracked vehicles pick up equipment directly from your facility. We manage the physical chain of custody from the moment we arrive. Nothing leaves your building unaccounted for.

GPS-Tracked Fleet
2

Audit & Inventory

Every serial number is scanned into our SCM system on arrival. You receive a full manifest. If it came in, it's accounted for — no exceptions, no gaps in the record.

SCM System Logged
3

Data Sanitization

Hard drives are handled to NIST 800-88 standards — degaussing, certified software wipe, or physical shredding depending on the security classification. You choose the level; we execute and certify it.

NIST 800-88 Certified
4

Value Engineering

Functional components are evaluated for the secondary market or routed to our HSLRK community initiative. You receive a Revenue Share Statement for anything remarketed. The rest is asset recovery, not waste.

Revenue Share Issued
5

Final Disposition

Non-functional materials are sorted into raw commodities — copper, steel, gold-bearing components — and routed to EPA-certified smelting partners. Your Certificate of Recycling closes the loop.

EPA-Certified Recycling

Service Offerings

What we actually do for you.

Four core services, built around the reality that IT disposition is a security problem, a legal problem, a financial problem, and a logistics problem — all at once.

01

On-Site Data Destruction

Mobile shredding and degaussing at your front door. Your data never leaves your facility before it's destroyed. We bring the equipment to you, not the other way around.

02

Data Center Decommissioning

Full-scale rack removal, cable mining, and infrastructure teardown. We handle the physical complexity of large-scale decommissions so your team doesn't have to.

IT Asset Remarketing

We identify and sell the components in your retired gear that still have market value — GPUs, CPUs, RAM, networking hardware. You receive a revenue share. Your old gear pays for your new gear.

Compliance Auditing
